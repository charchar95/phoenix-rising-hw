Friskies Sponsorship

Grumpy Cat became the Offical Spokescat of Friskies in September 2013. She made video appearances on the Friskies YouTube Game Show, trying out their toys and food.

Grumpy Cat Books

Grumpy Cat's first book was published on July 23, 2013, by Chronicle Books. Debuting at #8 hardcover nonficition on the the Publishers Weekly best seller list. Following this success, Grumpy Cat released a series of books including a comic book, stickers book, and tattoo books.

Films

Grumpy Cat made her first film appearance in April 2013 in Lil Bub and Friends, a documentary that premiered at the Tribeca Film Festival. In the Lifetime film, Grumpy Cat's Worst Christmas Ever, Aubrey Plaza voiced Grumpy Cat.
